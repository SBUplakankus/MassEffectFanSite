What is Mass Effect?

Mass Effect is a trilogy of video games developed by Bioware with the first installment being released in 2007. The series recieved critical acclaim and is one of the most well known video game franchises of all time. With a diverse and deep universe and a great cast of characters, many players have grown fond of this world and how it came to be.

How much Mass Effect is there?

Between the three games with all their DLC's there is well over 100+ hours of gameplay to enjoy inside the trilogy. This isn't including the vast universe and extra content such as books and Mass Effect themed mods for countless other games. There is so much more to experience once you have finished playing the games for the first time.
