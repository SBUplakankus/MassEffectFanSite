Mark Meer - Commander Shepard (Male)

Born in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada in 1971, Mark Meer is a writer, actor and improviser. He is known mainly for his roles in videos games as a voice actor for games produced by BioWare. He was a favourite with BioWare during their best years being given roles in all of their major games.

He has voiced characters in Jade Empire, the Dragon Age Series, and even Baldurs Gate 1 and 2, with his most recent role being Starro the Conqueror in Gotham Knights. What most don't know however is that he has done multiple roles inside of Mass Effect not just as Shepard. He voiced the Vorcha, Blasto and many other minor characters throughout the series.

Jennifer Hale - Commander Shepard (Female)

Jennifer Hale is a world renowned voice actor from Newfoundland and Labrador, Canada. She has been active as a voice actor since 1988, featuring in many roles through the years. Hale has been in countless animated TV Shows and movies varying from Comic Book heroes to Cartoon Network shows to Star Wars and Scooby Doo.

She was also featured in the very first Metal Hear Solid and Baldurs Gate games in 1998. She had also voiced Bastilla Shan in another legendary franchise made by BioWare, Knights of the Old Republic, before doing Mass Effect. Since doing Mass Effect she has also had big roles in games like Halo, The Elder Scrolls, and Mortal Kombat.
