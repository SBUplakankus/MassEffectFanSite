Mass Effect

Mass Effect 1 was released by BioWare in 2007 on the PS3, Xbox 360 and PC to critical acclaim. As the first game of the trilogy, it introduces the players to Commander Shepard, and the first iteration of his crew. Most of whom would go on to have featured roles in the series.

Set in the year 2183 we are introduced to a new universe where humanity are considered newcomers, looked down upon by the rest of the galaxy for their brash and unpredictable nature. While onboard the SSV Normandy, Shepard and his crew are sent to investigate the Human colony of Eden Prime.

The whole planet has gone dark shortly after scientists at the colony reported the discovery of a mysterious alien artifact. Under the watch of one of the councils infamous Spectres, the story of Commander Shepard begins here.
